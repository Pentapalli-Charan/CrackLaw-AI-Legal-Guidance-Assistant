THE AIR (PREVENTION AND CONTROL OF POLLUTION) ACT, 1981

CHAPTER I
PRELIMINARY

Section 1. Short title, extent and commencement.
(1) This Act may be called the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.
(2) It extends to the whole of India.

Section 2. Definitions.
In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,—
(a) "air pollutant" means any solid, liquid or gaseous substance present in the atmosphere.
(b) "air pollution" means the presence in the atmosphere of any air pollutant.
(c) "approved appliance" means any equipment or device used for the burning of any fuel.

Question: What is the penalty for air pollution under the Air Act 1981?
Answer: Under Section 37, whoever fails to comply with the provisions of section 21 or section 22 shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than one year and six months.

Context: Section 2(a) of the Air Act 1981
Question: What is the definition of air pollutant?
